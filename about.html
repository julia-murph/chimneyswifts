Dynamics of an urban Chimney Swift (Chaetura pelagica) roost system during autumn migration (2022)

The Wilson Journal of Ornithology | DOI: 10.1676/21-00081

This website was made in collaboration with Dr. Andrew Laughlin, assistant professor of Environmental Studies at UNC Asheville. The data for the maps and most of the information came from a season-long survey in 2020, conducted by Dr. Laughlin and two UNCA graduates, Blake Hudson and Torin Brewer-Jensen. Their research was published in the Wilson Journal of Ornithology in 2022.

New Media 374: Story Mapping

UNC Asheville | Julia Murphy

This website is a final project designed by Julia Murphy at UNC Asheville. The course is taught by Dr. Victoria Bradbury, who collaborated with other faculty members to collect data sets for her students to integrate into intuitive map-based websites.
